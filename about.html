My Work

Recent Accomplishments:



In recent years I expanded the HR department at SFFedCU to meet the growing people support needs. I also focused on creating a solid infrastructure to support this need, implementing a new and streamlined procedures for recruitment, hiring and onboarding which both improved the candidate selection process as well as cut down our recruitment average time by 72%(29 days). This effort was coupled by our Glassdoor campaign, creating an impactful employer presence and increasing our rating from 2.4 to 4.2.



We were very successful in implementing retention, rewards and recognition programs such as, Flexible Work Schedule (WFH) policy, Job Shadowing and Mentor Program. Recently our biggest focus was placed on implementing a new Performance Evaluation procedure which is tied directly to our annual merit increase and incentive schedule. What really helped with our staff's engagement and career development is the implementation of Advancement Criteria. With this program we created a development and advancement roadmap for all positions at the CU. Through the incorporation Performance Reviews and Advancement Criteria into our Compensation Administration Guidelines we incorporated career advancement as part of the rewards system. This system ensures compensation is no longer an off limits topic as the process and philosophy behind it are transparent. As head of HR with an analytical predisposition I conduct annual compensation analysis of our compensation bands, regularly scheduled merit increases, equity adjustment and timely promotions to ensure that we are living up to our objectives above and to create a feasible platform for fair and equitable pay. Because of this we have increased our internal recruitment (36%), retention (13%) and engagement.



In addition to improving the infrastructure within the HR department I took on a number of organizational projects that have helped straemline processes enabling a leaner operation. An example of this is the internal Parking Application for our employee parking lot which I had initiated, and managed to completion. This app allows the temporary request/reassignment of parking spaces temporarily not in use by currently assigned employees who are out of office. As a result of this implementation we have seen decreased commuter/parking reimbursement costs.
